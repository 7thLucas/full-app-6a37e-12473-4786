The Challenge

Pharma field teams are flying blind

✕

Reps carry printed brochures and fill call reports hours later — logs are incomplete, inconsistent, and non-compliant.

✕

Managers have no real-time view of HCP coverage, visit frequency, or what is actually being presented in the field.

✕

Sample distribution tracked on paper — creating inventory blind spots and compliance exposure at every audit.

The Solution

One app. Every detail. Instant record.

✓

Reps present compliance-approved digital content and submit a structured call report on the spot — before leaving the clinic.

✓

Managers see every visit instantly in a live dashboard — by rep, territory, product, and call frequency.

✓

Sample dispensing captured with digital signatures — full compliance record, zero paper trail, audit-ready.
