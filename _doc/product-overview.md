# MEGMA HEALTHCARE — Product Overview

## Product Name
**MEGMA HEALTHCARE**

## What It Is
A mobile-first digital detailing platform for pharmaceutical medical sales representatives (MSRs) to present drug and product information to healthcare professionals (HCPs) during field visits, log structured call reports on the spot, track sample dispensing, and give sales managers real-time visibility into territory performance — all from a single app. The platform ships with a visual aid content management system: Brand Managers can upload and organise product visual aids at any time after launch, and reps can present whatever is in the library immediately — no pre-loaded content is required to go live.

## The Problem
- MSRs rely on printed brochures and fill call reports manually hours after a visit — logs are often incomplete, inconsistent, or non-compliant
- Managers have no real-time view of HCP coverage, visit frequency, or what content is actually being presented in the field
- Compliance teams cannot verify what was presented to which doctor, when, or what samples were dispensed
- Sample distribution is tracked on paper, creating inventory blind spots and audit exposure

## The Solution
A structured digital detailing app where reps conduct and record every HCP visit from a single mobile tool: they present centrally managed, compliance-approved product slides, capture doctor feedback, log samples dispensed, and submit a call report before leaving the clinic. Managers see all activity in a live dashboard the moment it is logged.

## Primary Users (Personas)
1. **Medical Sales Representative (MSR)** — field rep conducting HCP detail visits daily; the primary app user
2. **Sales / Regional Manager** — monitors territory coverage, rep performance, call frequency, and call quality in real time
3. **Product / Brand Manager** — uploads, manages, and compliance-approves the detailing content and slide decks reps present

## Core Workflow
1. Rep opens the app and selects an HCP / doctor from their territory contact list
2. Presents the approved product slides in a structured detail session
3. Notes doctor interest level, feedback, and samples dispensed
4. Submits a structured call report before leaving the clinic
5. Visit data syncs instantly to the manager dashboard — visits, coverage, call stats by rep, territory, and product

## Key Features
- **Digital Product Presentations** — centrally managed, compliance-approved CLM slide decks presented directly from the app during each HCP visit
- **HCP Contact Management** — doctor profiles with full visit history, specialty, territory mapping, and engagement tracking
- **Call Report Builder** — structured in-app form for logging each visit's details, doctor feedback, interest level, and next steps — submitted on the spot
- **Sample Inventory Tracker** — real-time sample stock management, dispensing records, and digital compliance signatures; fully paperless and audit-ready
- **Manager Analytics Dashboard** — live view of visit frequency, territory coverage, rep performance, and call quality scores; no end-of-week lag
- **Offline Mode** — full app functionality in low-connectivity clinic environments; data syncs automatically on reconnect

## Brand & Tone
Professional, clinical, and clean. Projects pharmaceutical credibility and brand consistency in front of HCPs. Confident without being flashy — every interaction must feel approved, structured, and trustworthy.

## Platform
Mobile-first (tablet + smartphone) for field reps. Web dashboard for managers and brand managers.

## Strategic Principles
- Every detail visit is logged — no unrecorded HCP interactions
- Content is compliance-approved and centrally controlled before reps can present it
- Reporting is instant — reps spend time selling, not filling forms after hours
- Managers act on live field data, not end-of-week summaries

## Positioning
The go-to digital detailing tool for pharma field teams that want faster call cycles, cleaner compliance records, and real-time visibility from HQ to the last clinic visit.
