MEGMA HEALTHCARE is a mobile-first digital detailing platform for pharmaceutical medical sales representatives (MSRs) to present drug/product information to healthcare professionals (HCPs) during field visits, log structured call reports on the spot, track sample dispensing, and give sales managers real-time visibility into territory performance.

## Users
1. Medical Sales Representative (MSR) — field rep conducting HCP detail visits daily; primary app user
2. Sales / Regional Manager — monitors territory coverage, rep performance, call frequency, call quality in real time
3. Product / Brand Manager — uploads, manages, and compliance-approves detailing content and slide decks

## Core Workflow
1. Rep selects an HCP/doctor from their territory contact list
2. Presents approved product slides in a structured detail session
3. Notes doctor interest level, feedback, samples dispensed
4. Submits a structured call report before leaving the clinic
5. Visit data syncs instantly to the manager dashboard

## Key Features
- Digital Product Presentations (compliance-approved CLM slide decks)
- HCP Contact Management (doctor profiles, visit history, specialty, territory mapping)
- Call Report Builder (structured in-app form for visit details, feedback, interest level, next steps)
- Sample Inventory Tracker (real-time stock, dispensing records, digital compliance signatures, paperless/audit-ready)
- Manager Analytics Dashboard (live visit frequency, territory coverage, rep performance, call quality scores)
- Visual aid content management system (Brand Managers upload/organise product visual aids anytime; reps present immediately)

## Brand & Tone
Professional, clinical, clean. Pharmaceutical credibility and brand consistency. Confident without being flashy — every interaction must feel approved, structured, trustworthy.

## Platform
Mobile-first (tablet + smartphone) for field reps. Web dashboard for managers and brand managers.

## Strategic Principles
- Every detail visit is logged — no unrecorded HCP interactions
- Content is compliance-approved and centrally controlled before reps present it
- Reporting is instant — reps spend time selling, not filling forms after hours
- Managers act on live field data, not end-of-week summaries
